Pizza

About Pizza

The Meriam-Webster Dictionary defines pizza as, "A dish made typically of flattened bread dough spread with a savory mixture usually including tomatoes and cheese and often other toppings and baked."

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Basil
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Knead dough
  2. Let dough rise
  3. Preheat oven to max temperature
  4. Shape dough into circle
  5. Add tomato sauce to pizza
  6. Add cheese to pizza
  7. Add basil to pizza
  8. Add additional toppings to pizza
  9. Bake pizza for 5 - 10 minutes depending on temperature
